When I'm not programming, you can find me in our local not-for-profit Chattanooga, TN based pottery studio: Scenic City Clay Arts. From shaping forms on the wheel or by hand and experimenting with glazes, pottery constantly challenges me to appreciate the beauty inherent in the small imperfections that make us who we are.
The daughter of an architect and career artist, I find joy in bringing blank canvases to life with colors and design. My work can be found in the Grant Park Gallery and Buckhead Art Gallery in Atlanta, GA, and in private collections across the nations. Painting fuels my passion for self-expression and inspires new perspectives in everything I do.
Outside of work and play, I spend time volunteering for an organization that provides no-cost English instruction to women and girls in Kabul, Afghanistan, and serving as a sobreity mentor for patients at various inpatient mental health and addiction treatment centers in the Chattanooga, TN area.
